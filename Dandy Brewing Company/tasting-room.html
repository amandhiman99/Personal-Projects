What We Do
The Dandy Brewing Company and Tasting Room is a unique experience located in the heart of Ramsay. The modern space featurs 20 taps of Dandy Beers(maybe a few rad guest taps), a curated cocktail list featuring local spirit, a daily selection of top quality wines available by the glass.
Making the experience even better is a menu of drinking food, rootes in the fine dining. Chef Merritt Gordon has put together something amazing, enhancing the already indulgent experience.
Open Daily for lunch and dinner service, as well as brunch on weekends.

Restaurant
Offering a full lunch, dinner and brunch menu, the Tasting Room brings a unique drinking menu to the craft beer.
With snacks, plates, desserts, and a fresh oyster bar, the menu is meant to be shared among friends and lovers. Always evolving, the menu is perfectly paired with all things Dandy.
Vegeterain and Vegan options available. Please ask your server.

Shop
Dandy is pleased to offer a small offering of merchandise.
Along with T-shirts and hats, we are pleased to offer numbered silk screen prints, designed by Artists we are so pleased to call friends, and printed by team at Burnt Toast Studios.
We are also excited to announce a collaborative with North American Quality Purveyors on a line of T- shirts and baseball caps. We worked together to design custom cut shirts and hats, that fit a Dandy perfectly.
