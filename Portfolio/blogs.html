When I first do watched the movie Interstellar
I am always fan of the science and especially the space explorations. On March 22, 2016, I first watched the movie interstellar directed by Christopher Nolan. This movie is about saving the mankind from the consequences of the global warming and dying crops. This movie has the most real simulation of the black hole which was discovered to be exactly the same as the real blackhole found by the Event Horizon Telescope. The cooper station and the robots and these were the most advanced a human can get. Though the movie is quite confusing but I like the facts that on different planets the simulate the environment like wise. If someone can notice on Dr. Miller's water world their is a ticking sound every 1.5 seconds which means by each ticking sound a year on earth passes by. The lead hero 'Joseph Cooper' when he fell into the black hole, and then he can see the most complex 4 dimensional structure made by some advance civilisation. There he can see the past and present at the same time. The thing which fascinated me was how he can communicate with his daughter Murph through different means using morse code as well.
